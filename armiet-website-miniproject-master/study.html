Study

Classrooms

The Theatres of Knowledge.

The multitude of budding bachelor,masters of engineers and management students study in 120+ classrooms at any point of time.

All classrooms at Armiet campuses are spacious and most of them are amphitheatre style. They provide the most conducive atmosphere for dynamic and focused discussions. Classrooms at Armiet have been designed to bring together analysis with action and are augmented with integrated audio-visual teaching aids for lectures, presentations etc.

Library

If you have a garden and a library, you have everything you need. - Marcus Tullius Cicero

The libraries at Armiet are more like Integrated Knowledge Resource Centres that are stocked with over 60 thousands plus books in total; periodicals, references, national and international journals, covering all aspects of academic studies and research materials. Students can take benefits of books as long as they wants at free of cost. e Library installed 20 computers with all specifications for e-learning for the purpose of student and faculty. The Library is having 40,850 volume of books in academic, technical, and other reference books. Project Reports which are regularly updated for references by the students. ARMIET library has also collection of CDs/Audio/Videos for use of the Students. ARMIET library has the facility of daily news papers, periodicals etc. The number of national magazines are 50 and the number of international magazines are 10. The Institute has 2000 + ebooks and national as well as international academic journals including IEEE, ASME, Springer, McGraw Hill Digital Library, Elsevier and J-Gate. The Institute has subscribed to Del-Net for obtaining E-Journals, CD ROms, books, current periodicals, union catalogues , Data Base of Indian Specialists, Data Base of Theses and Dissertations using GIST technology. ALL the DELNET databases have been resident on DELSIS, an in-house software developed on BASISPlus, an RBMS, the product of information dimensions Inc. of USA., and provides an array facilities to all the Libraries in all over the country. The Library has the facility to access NPTEL videos. The Library has the collection of last 3 years questions papers along with answer booklets solved. Facility of career guidance, examinations solved papers also available. A Xerox Centre, and other stationery facility is available next to the Library.
