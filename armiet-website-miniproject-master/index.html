WHY ARMIET?

Focus on Developing students as an Entrepreneur & just not Teaching for Degree purpose. Regular sessions on personality development to groom students as Corporate Leaders. Our Placement cell helps students to get the Right jobs for the Right candidate. We also Conduct sessions on Pre-Placement Talks, Personal Interviews and Group Discussions. Paid Internship is provided to Each Student in The first year it self for few months so that they are Equipped with Requirement of Industry. Our Pedagogy includes Presentations, Role play, Case Study, Self Learning Video Presentations & Field activities. Conduct Psychometric Tests such as Self Esteem Test, G.K.Test, Aptitude Test & Emotional Intelligence Test for each Student with a Feed Back of Improvement.
